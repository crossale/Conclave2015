Awaken Our Past - Define Our Future

The past is a treasure trove of information and the last 100 years have offered up many lessons to consider as we move forward. Therefore, it is only right that the spirit of this theme encourages us to awaken the burning embers of history to instill new life in our order.

While the lessons of the past are important to reflect upon, preparation for the future starts now. Idleness is useless and in order to improve our order and even scouting, we must be striving ever onward.

About

This coming conclave is still in the planning stages but right now we can tell you with great excitement that it will be hosted at the beautiful Resica Falls Scout Reservation from May 29-31. It is a once in a lifetime event where you can celebrate this once-in-a-lifetime occasion with your brothers.

Planning is in full swing but of course all of the pieces are not quite together. Stay tuned by following up here, signing up for our newsletter above, or by using social media. As program planning continues, we will make sure to keep you up to date with the information you need.

Sign Up

Members of lodges of NE-5B need to register through the lodge they are associated with. You can reach out to your lodge leadership by following any one of the links on the Lodges page of NE-5B.

All other members can register through Unami Lodge's provisional contingent form. Any and all questions about this can be directed to our conclave committee.
