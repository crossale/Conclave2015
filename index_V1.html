Awaken Our Past - Define Our Future

The past is a treasure trove of information and the last 100 years have offered up many lessons to consider as we move forward. Therefore, it is only right that the spirit of this theme encourages us to awaken the burning embers of history to instill new life in our order.

While the lessons of the past are important to reflect upon, preparation for the future starts now. Idleness is useless and in order to improve our order and even scouting, we must be striving ever onward.

About
